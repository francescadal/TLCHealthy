About Us

For more than 43 years, TLC Healthy Communities has never underestimated the ability of young people to overcome challenges and heal, learn, and flourish. Our private nonprofit provides an array of medical, educational, and daily living services designed to maximize the independence of those with special health care needs. The Center provides a network of care for individuals at all stages of development, from the most technologically-dependent and medically fragile to those with mild developmental delays. From premature infants to young adults, we are focused on developing and delivering the very best care necessary to ensure each individual is given every opportunity to thrive and succeed. By listening to and learning from the families we partner with, the Center has grown from a single program in 1971 to one that improves thousands of lives through a dozen programs serving the tri-county area, the state, and nation. Our mission is to respond to the unique needs of every child through programs that promote privacy, respect, independence, dignity, and engagement (PRIDE).

Programs

Children and Youth Sevices

Tender Loving Care Healthy Communities works to assist youth, young adults, elders and families, using a coherent approach to improve the overall social, educational, and economic development. We work towards our mission in the following ways:

  • Empowering residents of South Florida to become self-sufficient, through literacy, computer education, job training and education.
  • Welcoming newly arrived immigrants and orienting them to their new home.
  • Helping elders get access to housing, healthcare, and all the benefits they deserve.
  • Supporting immigrants youth as they struggle to integrate into the American society without losing their own unique and cherished culture.
  • Referring clients to legal, medical, and social services as they need arise.

Get Involved

There are many meaningful ways to get involved and make a difference for the children and adults served by TLC Healthy Communities. From utilizing the services of TLC Healthy Communities three mission-based enterprises, providing internships or jobs for our students, to volunteering your time to help a child in our preschool, there is a variety of different ways you can connect with our mission no matter your age, schedule or talents.

Volunteer

Volunteers have an enormous impact on the health and well-being of the individuals and families served by TLC Healthy Communities. Those who volunteer their talents also contribute significantly to the growth and long-term viability of the organization. Just some of the various volunteer opportunities include:

  • Hands-on, administrative, grounds and facilities work.
  • Direct service, mentoring, tutoring and caring for individuals with disabilities.
  • Student community projects
  • Group projects/work days (corporate, religious groups, etc.)

Advocacy

TLC Healthy Communities is committed to helping promote and protect the human rights of individuals with developmental disabilities. We ask that you to join us to help ensure that this vulnerable population is protected. Here are just a few ways you can help advocate on behalf of children and adults with disabilities:

  • Write a letter to your elected official asking that they support funding for services that help individuals with disabilities get the supports they need to remain independent.
  • Follow TLC Healthy Communities on Facebook and share the great work that the agency is doing for individuals with developmental disabilities.
  • Actively support the inclusion and participation of individuals with disabilities in the community and throughout their lifetimes by employing, mentoring and befriending them.
